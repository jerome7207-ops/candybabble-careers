      How Your Data Is Used
    

    

      
Accessibility preferences are used only to help filter and prioritize job listings. They are not included in any employer-facing resume and are not shared with employers, third parties, or advertisers.

      
Resume data you save is stored in your personal account and used only to generate tailored resume versions. It is not shared or sold.

      
Data protections using Supabase Row Level Security ensure your data is accessible only under your own authenticated session.

      
You remain in control. All selections are optional. You can update, clear, or request deletion of any data at any time.
